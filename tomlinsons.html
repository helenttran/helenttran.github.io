Tomlinson's Pet Feed

During Spring 2019, my team and I were tasked with creating a media plan for a local Austin pet store, Tomlinson's Pet Feed. This media plan was to move the the store into the Ballard neighborhood of Seattle. This was a 15-week long project that was divided into three parts: "Research & Insights," "Recommendations & Logistics," and a presentation. Our team, Metamorphosis Media, won the best presentation.
